Day 1: Welcome to Parenthood

Tip of the Day: Take it one moment at a time. Your baby's basic needs are simple: feeding, sleeping, diapering, and lots of love.

Milestone Watch: Your newborn may recognize your voice from their time in the womb.

Self-Care Reminder: Accept help when it's offered - you don't have to do this alone.

Day 7: First Week Milestone

Tip of the Day: Your baby's umbilical cord stump will fall off around this time. Keep the area clean and dry.

Development Note: Your baby may turn their head toward familiar sounds and voices.

Parent Reminder: You're doing great! Every baby is different, and you're learning together.

Day 30: One Month Achievement

Tip of the Day: Your baby might start making eye contact and responding to faces.

Activity Suggestion: Try simple face-to-face interactions during alert periods.

Wellness Check: Schedule your baby's 1-month pediatrician visit if you haven't already.
